Chapter 1: Unwanted Attractions

Callie meets Anson, her brother’s best friend, who is working as a contractor building her home. Despite his imposing character Callie experiences an unexpected fascination.
When Callie is threatened by unknown forces, Anson’s protective instincts take over. Their connection evolves as they spend more time togethersuitor. The two get closer as they share their personal tales and vulnerabilities. Callie begins to rely on Anson for emotional support Accidents near Callie’s house intensify, suggesting a targeted threat. Anson’s protecting presence becomes increasingly important as they uncover the source of the threats. Callie discovers buried mysteries about her family and past, confusing her emotions and raising the stakes in their predicament. They work together to solve the mysteries surrounding the attacks. The horrific truth, which involves people close to them, is more than they could have imagined A risky showdown with the adversary characterizes the climax. Callie and Anson rely on mutual trust and cooperation to defeat the menace. The novel Fragile Sanctuary masterfully combines romance and suspense.
